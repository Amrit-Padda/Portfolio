About Us

What is BitKye?

BitKye is safe and secure way to pool funds and fund your goals

How does it work?

We leverage the Bitcoin Blockchain to process payments and distribute pool funds. Simply transfer the pool buy in amount to the BitKye address and that's it! You're automatically added to the next queue. 

Whats the catch?

When you decide to join a pool, you will have to enter an amount greater than the pool buy in amount. Any amount over the pool buy in amount is distributed to the other pool members as interest, highest bid wins!

Whats in it for me?

Great question! If you're in need of funds, you can bid on a pool and take home the amount. It's that easy! BitKye operates on an honor model, we expect that once you've achieved your goals, you will return and help others achieve their goals too!

Interested in investing?

Sure you are! BitKye offers exceptional returns when contributing to a pool. Simply contribute the minimum amount to a pool, and collect interest when another member collects a pool. Once you've reached your target, simply bid on a pool and recuperate your principal. It's that easy!
